# Office Hours

This folder contains slides and materials from the RSpace open source office hours — a bi-monthly community call open to anyone interested in RSpace, its development, and the broader research data management ecosystem.

## What are office hours?

Office hours are a regular space to connect with the RSpace core team and community. Sessions typically cover project updates, roadmap discussions, contributor spotlights, and open Q&A. No agenda or prior experience required — everyone is welcome.

Upcoming sessions and registration links are listed in the [Community Calendar](../the%20rspace%20project/Guide/calendar.md).
