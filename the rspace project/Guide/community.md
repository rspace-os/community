## Community Calendar

Upcoming and notes from past events can be found on our [Community Calendar](calendar.md)

## Open Source Office Hours

Our current primary community gathering is the **bimonthly office hours** where the RSpace team and community come together to discuss progress, share ideas, and connect.

### What Happens in Office Hours?

- **Updates**: Latest releases, roadmap changes, and project news
- **Demos**: New features, integrations, and community projects
- **Q&A**: Ask questions, get help, discuss ideas
- **Collaboration**: Meet other contributors and users
- **Feedback**: Share your experiences and requests

### Schedule

- **Frequency**: Every 2 months 
- **Duration**: 55 minutes
- **Format**: Virtual (video call)

**Next Session**: Check our [Community Calendar](calendar.md) for the next scheduled office hour and instructions on how to join

## Community Meetings

We currently don't have any regular conferences or meet ups organized by ResearchSpace, but there are self-organised gatherings like the annual RSpace Germany meet up.

If you have an idea or are already planning any events or workshops around RSpace, **we'd love to hear it** and add it to the community calendar.

## Connect with Us Online

Find all the ways to connect and stay up to date with us online, e.g. [Mastodon, LinkedIn, Bluesky, newsletter and more](contact.md) .

## Connect with Us at Conferences & Events

The ResearchSpace team regularly presents at research data management, open science, and domain-specific conferences. Our contributions are not always about RSpace, but it is a great opportunity to meet the team in person and we're happy to chat about RSpace all the time. See the full calendar [here](https://www.researchspace.com/community#events).
