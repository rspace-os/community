# Team & Governance

## Who Maintains RSpace

The RSpace open-source project is maintained by [ResearchSpace](https://researchspace.com), the organization that develops and supports RSpace as an open-source platform and provides enterprise services around it.

### Core Team

[Official team page](https://www.researchspace.com/about)

### Community Contributors

We're grateful to growing community of contributors including:

- Integration builders connecting RSpace with other tools
- Documentation writers improving guides and tutorials
- Researchers providing feedback and feature ideas

## Governance Model

### Decision Making

RSpace uses a **maintainer-led governance model**. 

**Community input** actively shapes decisions through:
- Office hours discussions
- GitHub issues 
- Direct communication with maintainers
- Feature proposals and use case sharing
- Collaborative projects with other tool developers and open source communities

The **ResearchSpace team** has final decision authority on:
- Roadmap and strategic direction of the rspace-os project
- Feature prioritization
- Architectural decisions
- Release schedules
- Code acceptance

### Why This Model?

As an organization supporting RSpace in production environments, ResearchSpace takes responsibility for:
- Long-term sustainability and maintenance
- Quality and security standards
- Enterprise customer commitments
- Integration with research infrastructure

This structure ensures stability while remaining open to community innovation.

### Future Evolution

As the community grows, we may evolve further governance to include:
- Community advisory board
- Working groups for specific areas (integrations, UX, etc.)
- Community maintainers with commit access
- More formalized RFC process for major changes
