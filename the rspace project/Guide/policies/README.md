# Policies & Governance

The RSpace project operates under a set of policies that ensure a healthy, legal, and sustainable open-source project and community.

## Organization-Level Policies

Most of the policies below are maintained at the **organization level** in the [rspace-os/.github repository](https://github.com/rspace-os/.github) and apply to all RSpace repositories, unless otherwise stated


## Policy Documents

### [Code of Conduct](code-of-conduct.md)
Community behavior guidelines and enforcement processes. **Required reading for all contributors.**

### [Licenses & CLA](licenses.md)
Software licensing (AGPL 3.0) and Contributor License Agreement requirements.

### [Security Policy](security.md)
How to report security vulnerabilities and our response process.

### [Trademarks](trademarks.md)
Guidelines for using RSpace name, logo, and trademarks.

## Policy Updates

Policies may be updated to:
- Reflect community growth
- Address new situations
- Improve clarity
- Align with best practices

**Major changes** will be communicated through:
- Office hours announcements
- Newsletter
- This guide
