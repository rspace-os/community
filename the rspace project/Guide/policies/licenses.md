## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

### What is a CLA?

A **Contributor License Agreement** is a legal document that you sign when contributing to RSpace. It establishes rights and responsibilities for your contributions.

**→ [Read the full CLA](https://github.com/rspace-os/.github/CLA.md)** 

### Why is a CLA Required?

The CLA:

- ✅ **Protects you**: Confirms you have the right to contribute
- ✅ **Protects the project**: Ensures RSpace can use and distribute your contribution
- ✅ **Maintains open source**: Keeps licensing clear and consistent
- ✅ **Provides legal clarity**: Reduces uncertainty about intellectual property

### Individual CLA

**When you contribute** as an individual:
- You retain copyright to your contribution
- You grant the RSpace project rights to use your contribution
- You confirm you have the legal right to contribute
- You agree contributions are under AGPL 3.0

**What you grant**:
- Perpetual, worldwide license to use your contribution
- Ability to distribute under AGPL 3.0

**What you keep**:
- Copyright ownership
- Ability to use your code elsewhere

### Corporate CLA (CCLA)

**If contributing on behalf of your employer or when you're using your employer's time and resources**:

- Your company may need to sign a Corporate CLA first
- Confirms whether company or employee owns the contribution
- Typically Grants same rights as Individual CLA
- Protects both you and your employer

**Not sure whether you need a CCLA for your contribution?** Contact your employer's legal department.

A typical CCLA can be found below, but often these need to be adjusted for the individual contribution scenario.

**→ [See a typical CCLA](https://github.com/rspace-os/.github/CCLA.md)** 

### How to Sign the CLA

**Note:** your employer might require signing a CCLA (corporate CLA) first before you can follow the process outlined below.

**Process**:
1. **Submit your first pull request**
2. **CLA bot will comment** on your PR
3. **Click the CLA link** in the bot comment
4. **Confirm with a reply** to the bot comment that your have read and agree with the CLA
5. **CLA Bot will update** once you've signed
6. **Your PR can now be reviewed** for merging

### CLA Status

**Once signed**:
- ✅ Valid for all future contributions
- ✅ You won't need to sign again

**Checking your status**:
- CLA bot will confirm on each PR
